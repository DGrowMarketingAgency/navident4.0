NaviVoice – Voice-Guided Workflow

Feature:

NaviVoice enables surgeons to control the navigation system entirely with voice commands, keeping their hands sterile and focused on the procedure.

Benefits:

  • Hands-free operation improves surgical efficiency.
  • Reduces interruptions, allowing continuous focus on patient care.
  • Minimizes cross-contamination risks during procedures.

Use Cases:

  • Implant surgeries where both hands are required for precision drilling.
  • Complex cases where switching between tools/screens can cause delays.
  • Teaching environments, where verbal commands showcase workflow to students.
